Life Story

I was born in Winter Springs, Florida on March 25th, 1995. Growing up, I had one sister and two step-brothers. I've always been interested in computers and electronics of all sorts since I was young. Mom says I could start the computer and load up my favorite game all by myself by the time I was a year old.


Music got me more into computers later on — using FrostWire to fill up my MP3 player, and later when my friends and I started a band, I had to learn even more software and hardware. In my teens, I discovered Raspberry Pi’s and Linux OS, which got me interested in automation and making simple mundane tasks easier or obsolete through programming.


Only recently have I truly begun to realize the power and potential of what computers can accomplish.
